ShopIT is an online marketplace in Awka where you can advertise products for sale and purchase products available on the platform. We provide you with products you can trust at the best rates. You can have your products delivered directly to you with a charge for delivery fee and make all payments via transfer or cash on delivery. Shopping online is easy and convenient with ShopIT.

How to place an order

  • Step 1: Click on desired product
  • Step 3: Take a screenshot
  • Step 4: Click Order
  • Step 5: Send screenshot

How to become a seller

  • Step 1: Contact Me
  • Step 2: Take a good picture of the product with plain background(preferably white)
  • Step 3: Send picture to contact above with the following details;
    • 1. Your business name
    • 2. Details about the product
    • 3. Price of product

Note: Seller must be based in Awka

Terms and conditions apply

Tips for sellers

  • 1. Reply ASAP when you receive a message requesting for your product to sell your products faster
  • 2. Don't scare customers away with your price
